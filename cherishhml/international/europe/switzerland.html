Switzerland

The scenic landscape of Switzerland is one of the world’s Must See tourist destinations. Tourists are drawn to Switzerland's Alpine climate and landscapes, in particular for skiing and mountaineering. This beautiful country is a paradise for honeymooners. From romancing old school style in horse carriages at Matterhorn to going for a luxury cruise ride at Lake Geneva, there is no dearth of romantic experiences in this quaint country. You will be enchanted by the country’s affable nature. The rich heritage, unique culture and lingual diversity marks this country as one of the most charming and unique country. beautiful mountains are especially inviting in the summer, when the cool Swiss air can make you believe in Paradise. The country is blessed with gorgeous hills, cascading waterfalls, fruit loaded orchards and green blossoming gardens .The copious beauty of nature is remarkable. With about 21 chocolate factories scattered around the country, it is Mecca for chocolate lovers. The weekly fleas of Geneva are to die for. Talking about food, the cuisine of Switzerland is deeply inspired by Italy, Germany and France. It offers spectacular views of paranomic Alps.
