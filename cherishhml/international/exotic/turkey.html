Turkey

Turkey is a nation straddling eastern Europe and western Asia with cultural connections to ancient Greek. Turkey is one of the leading tourism destinations in the world with its stunning scenery and rich historic legacy. Diverse offerings such as the ancient ruins of Ephesus to the luxury beach resorts along the Aegean Sea will enthrall and captivate even the most jaded traveler. Turkey offers activities to suit every temperament, from outdoors adventure to cultural enrichment. Turkey is a modern country with a captivating blend of antiquity and contemporary and East and West. Turkey is one of the fastest growing tourism destinations in the world, a truly fascinating country, culturally and historically, a great place to visit. Beautiful beaches, outdoor activities, natural wonders, blue voyage tours, historical places and religious sites, shopping alternatives, entertainment and nightlife, world heritage sites and more. Turkey has so many fantastic things to do and see for visitors.
