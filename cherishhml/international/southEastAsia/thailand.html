Thailand

Thailand is the country in Southeast Asia most visited by tourists, and for good reason. With astonishingly great food, a tropical climate, fascinating culture, majestic mountains and great beaches, Thailand is a must visit for travellers around the world. This South-east Asian country is best known for its sensual beaches, ancient architecture, ornate temples, royal palaces and much more. Thailand is now pretty easy and convenient with well-connected flights from all the major international and national airports. The unique Thai culture and the spicy Thai food with lots of cocktails will offer you the time of time as you can find open-air nightclubs located just beside some enchanting waterfalls. The place is where people stay in harmony with nature and yet have a lot of fun. Shopping in Thailand is hands-down one of the premier activities for visitors to the Kingdom. Some favorite items for shopping in Thailand are Thai silk, leather goods, silver & gold, precious and semi-precious gems, pearls, furniture, antiques, and all sorts of exotic handicrafts. You can enjoy a variety of featured theatrical arts that are common in Bangkok, Chiang Mai, Phuket, and Pattaya.
