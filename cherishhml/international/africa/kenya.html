Kenya

Kenya is a country in East Africa with coastline on the Indian Ocean. It has a beautiful mix of natural beauty and modernization with jaw-dropping vistas and high-rise metropolitan areas. It's also home to wildlife like lions, elephants and rhinos. From Nairobi, the capital, safaris visit the Maasai Mara Reserve, known for its annual wildebeest migrations, and Amboseli National Park. There has been a lot of growth in the past few years in Kenyaâ€™s tourism. Last year, it welcomed more than 2 million tourists, This is why the country takes exceptional care about tourism. Kenya has a tropical climate moderated by altitude. It's hot and humid at the coast, temperate inland, and very dry in the north and northeast parts. The best time to visit Kenya is during July through September as this is when the great migration takes places and one can witness wildebeests in huge numbers. Kenya is a country of epic landforms that stir our deepest longings for this very special continent.
