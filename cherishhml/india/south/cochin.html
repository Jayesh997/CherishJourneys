Cochin

Cochin is a commercial center and a harbour town in Ernakulam District of Kerala. The city can be regarded as the commercial and industrial capital of Kerala. The main attraction in Cochin is its serene backwaters, beautiful lagoons fringed by coconut palms and picturesque islands which brings many tourist’s every year. It’s a delightful place to explore, laze in arty cafes and relax at some of India’s finest homestays and heritage hotels. It's also an important centre for Keralan arts. The architecture and historical sites in Fort Kochi are the biggest draws for most visitors. It is one of the major tourist destinations in India.
