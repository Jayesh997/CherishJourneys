Goa

Goa trip is a perfect destination for the ones who need and want relaxation. Goa is blessed with wide and wonderful tourist spots like beaches, adventure, sports, churches, river Cruising, forts, heritage houses, lakes, springs, museums, temples, waterfalls and wildlife. Lavishly gifted by nature, Goa boasts of a rich cultural and historical heritage. It has a unique mix of Indian and Portuguese cultures and architecture. Goa enjoys a maritime climate, with April and May being very hot. The Goa Beach Resorts sitauted on the beach sides are the best way to enjoy Goa. Some of the famous tourist destinations of Goa holiday are Panaji, the capital city of Goa which lies near the end of the road from Mumbai to Goa, Arambol, Canacona, Mapusa, Margao, Panaji, Ponda and Vasco-da-Gama. Goa is also famous for adventure tourism like swimming, water sports, banana boat, windsurfing, jet skiing, parasailing, sea fishing, scuba diving, water scootering, water skiing, golfing, beach volleyball, beach basket ball, beach combing, making sand castles etc.
