Himachal Pradesh

Himachal brings to you a wide range of accommodation options to choose from to make sure you take home great and ‘comfy’ memories. Himachal Tourism is all about exploring the unexplored natural jewels of beauty that adorn the land of Himachal Pradesh. Dotted with numerous hill stations and sights of attraction that boasts snow-covered mountains, lush green valleys, oak & pine covered forests, pristine environs and natural peace. Despite the difficult terrain and varying climatic conditions, Himachal is well connected by roads, railways and air. The fascinating landscape presents an enchanting locale of mighty snow peaks, deep gorges, lush green valleys, flowery meadows, misty woods, flowing icy rivers, glaciers, shimmering lakes, spectacular waterfalls and enthralling lakes. It is a wonderful location for the tourists who want to relax and simply feel rejuvenated. The state stands apart in terms of its sheer topographic diversity and breath taking natural beauty. The Himachali land possesses such spellbinding magnetism that it attracts nature lovers from across the globe. The state also offers amazing opportunities for trekking, mountaineering, para gliding, skiing, golf , river rafting and ice skating.
