About US

We are very excited to introduce Cherish Journeys as the one stop provider for all your travel needs and requirements. We label ourselves as leading, innovative, and highly efficient and look forward to demonstrating these qualities at the earliest possible chance. Our primary aim is to provide quality travel services to business and leisure travellers. The agency continued to grow and branch out its interests to rise as one of the exponents in the travel industry. Our team of professionals will do everything possible to ensure that your holiday in India & Abroad are nicely arranged and in order. Our specialty, besides excellent tour and travel services is to give you an opportunity to participate in bespoke travel plans. If you would like a tour that is tailored exactly like the way you want it, you found the right company. Send us a request and we would be happy to assist!!

Why Cherish Journeys

We take great care to ensure that our tours are the best value for money in the business, and we know that outstanding value means ensuring that all the ingredients are right, not just the price. At Cherish Journeys we pride ourselves on providing a service with a personal touch that tailors every vacation to your own specific tastes, needs and budget. Many of the inclusions in our tours are the result of years of careful research to get the right balance of quality and adventure that is best suited to our travellers. Our clients’ satisfaction is our priority. That is why we work only with the best transport companies, qualified tour-guides, hotels and restaurants. If you are travelling to different destinations, you certainly don't want any expensive surprises or extra costs that you weren't told about before you booked your tour. Cherish Journeys is one of few up-front travel companies that tell you what is included and excluded in our cost rather than after you have booked your tour. Wherever you need us, we are always there for you. You can easily contact us on e-mail or by phone, follow us on Facebook and other social media platforms. We are happy to have you as a part of our community.
